Step 1: Power On Your Smart Wardrobe

Let's set up your Smart Wardrobe device:

  1. Plug in your Smart Wardrobe device to a power source.
  2. Wait until the indicator light starts flashing blue (approximately 30 seconds).
  3. The device is now ready for setup.
The flashing blue light indicates that your device is in setup mode.

Step 2: Generate Device Key

Now we'll generate a unique key for your device:

Step 3: Connect to Device WiFi

Connect your computer or phone to the device's WiFi network:

WiFi Name: Smartwardrobe-setup

Password: smartwardrobe123

Once connected to the device's WiFi:

  1. Your device should automatically show a setup page (or navigate to http://192.168.4.1)
  2. Enter your home WiFi name and password
  3. Enter the device key you generated in the previous step
  4. Click "Connect" on the device setup page
The device will use these credentials to connect to your home WiFi network.
